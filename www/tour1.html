Wallet

First thing you need is a wallet to identify yourself against a server node and to manage your coins

  • Your wallet is provided in a file generated in your own device when you request it
  • The file contains two keys: public and private
  • Each coin you mine and each transfer you send or receive is identified by your public key
  • To transfer coins, your private key is used to sign your transfer
  • We talk later about these. For now what you need to keep in mind is:
    • You can share your public key with anyone, for example to receive transactions
    • You should never share your private key or some one may impost you. For this reason the wallet file is created in your own device and never shared; not even with the server.
    • Keep the wallet safe

Press 'Create' to generate your wallet.
Your public key will be displayed below and your wallet saved in a file.
